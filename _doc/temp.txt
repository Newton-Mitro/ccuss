# 30-Day English Speaking Plan for Software Engineers

## Goal

By the end of 30 days, you should be able to:

* Introduce yourself confidently.
* Explain your projects and technical skills.
* Participate in meetings.
* Communicate with international teammates.
* Handle basic technical interviews in English.

## Daily Routine (30 Minutes)

### Step 1: Read Aloud (10 minutes)

* Read the day's sentences slowly.
* Focus on pronunciation and clarity.

### Step 2: Speak Without Reading (10 minutes)

* Close the text and try to say the sentences from memory.

### Step 3: Record Yourself (10 minutes)

* Record your voice on your phone.
* Listen and identify mistakes.
